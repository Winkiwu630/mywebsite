Website Projects

These are three real websites built from scratch. No page builders, drag-and-drop tools, templates, and most importantly no monthly subscriptions. Each one shows a different level of web project, at a different price point, with different capabilities.

Browse through them and see which one sounds like what you have in mind. The descriptions below each site are written in plain English. No tech jargon. The goal is for you to be able to look at these and say "yeah, that's what I need" or "no, I need more than that" before you ever reach out.

If you're still not sure, get in touch and we'll figure it out.

One-Page Site  |  Starting at $600

Homefront Repair Co. | Mock Handyman Site  Demo

Stack: HTML  ·  CSS  ·  Vanilla JavaScript

homefrontrepair.com

Good for you if... your goal is simple: people find you, see what you do, and get in touch. One page, anchor scroll navigation, a contact form - done. No appointment calendar, no product catalog, no logins. This is the professional online equivalent of a business card, except it works 24/7 and shows up on Google.

Best for: freelancers, tradespeople, local service businesses, real estate agents, anyone testing an idea before committing to more.

  • Contact form that sends directly to your email, no database needed
  • Works on phones, tablets, and desktops
  • Nothing to maintain after launch, no platform to log into, no monthly fees
  • Your only ongoing cost: a domain name (~$15/year)

Full Web Application  |  Starting at $2,500

C&C by Jordan & Aurora | Mock Salon Site  Demo

Stack: HTML  ·  CSS  ·  Vanilla JavaScript  ·  GitHub Pages

candcsalon.com

Good for you if... your customers need to actually do something on your site, not just read it. Book an appointment, check a calendar, browse services, fill out a multi-step form. This is where web development gets complex - there's real JavaScript logic running underneath everything you see. The demo here handles everything in the browser. A real working version of this would need a backend (a server and database) to actually save bookings and send confirmation emails - that's included in the price range.

Best for: salons, restaurants, service businesses that take appointments, contractors, anyone who needs their customers to be able to interact with the site rather than just look at it.

  • 5 pages: Home, Services, Booking flow, Calendar, Gallery
  • Flip-card service browser with deep links directly into the booking flow
  • 5-step booking flow with JavaScript state management and form validation
  • Interactive monthly calendar with stylist filtering and a day detail modal
  • Scroll-triggered animations, animated stat counters, auto-scrolling photo strip
  • Fully mobile-optimized throughout
  • A real production version would add a backend to store bookings and send emails
